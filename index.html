Privacy Policy for GymPlanner

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

GymPlanner is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

GymPlanner does NOT collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data. All data is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All workout data, including:

  • Exercise names and details
  • Sets, reps, and weights
  • Training groups
  • Photos you add
  • Notes

...is stored locally on your iPhone/iPad and never leaves your device.

Photos

When you add photos to exercises, GymPlanner accesses your photo library only with your permission. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

No Third-Party Services

GymPlanner does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Data Deletion

You can delete any data at any time within the app. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

GymPlanner does not knowingly collect any information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: fastcashestates@gmail.com
